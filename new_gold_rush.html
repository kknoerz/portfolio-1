Trevor Lazarus

New Gold Rush

Client: Visit California
Role: Designer & Developer 

Utilizing the narrative of three native celebrities’ love of the state and its varied amenities, this site sought to increase travel to California’s more luxurious hot spots.

Photography was a major asset for this campaign, but not every subject had specific visuals for use. This slideshow was a mobile-friendly solution to provide context without diminishing user experience.

Multiple story elements in a given talent’s narrative required a diversity of options, including a variety of slideshows in a given influencer’s story.
